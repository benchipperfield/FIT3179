World Food Consumption

A visualisation of food consumption over the globe.

Vegetables, Meat and GDP

In general, increasing GDP per capita increases a countries meat consumption and slightly increases vegetable consumption. Countries with extremely low GDP per capita tend to have low vegetable and meat consumption, and are likely countries in poverty.
